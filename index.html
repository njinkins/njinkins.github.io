About Me

Natalie Jinkins is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Geography with a specialization in Medical Geography in Global Health from the University of Florida’s College of Liberal Arts and Sciences. She is also minoring in Sociology and pursuing a certificate in Geospatial Information Analysis. Natalie was also able to partake in an exchange program during the Spring 2020 semester where she was able to study at Royal Holloway: University of London for three months, until the COVID-19 pandemic ended the program.

Throughout her studies at the University of Florida, Natalie has also been involved with numerous groups and societies, and she has gained valuable work experience. For example, Natalie is a part of the University of Florida’s Phi Eta Sigma Honor Society and was their most active member during the Fall 2020 semester. Additionally, Natalie has been a student intern at the Bureau of Economic and Business Research since the summer of 2020, and she has gained copious amounts of valuable work experience through the use of ArcMap, Excel, and various other programs.
